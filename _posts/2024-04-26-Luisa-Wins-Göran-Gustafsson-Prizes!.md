We are incredibly proud to announce that **Luisa W. Hugerth** has been awarded the prestigious **Göran Gustafsson Prize in Medical Science** at Uppsala University!

## About the Göran Gustafsson Prizes

The Göran Gustafsson Prizes are among Sweden's most prestigious scientific awards, recognizing outstanding researchers in various fields. This year's recipients in Medical Science are:

- **Luisa W. Hugerth** - For her groundbreaking work in microbiome research
- **Wojciech Michno** - For his contributions to medical science

## Recognition of Excellence

This award recognizes Luisa's exceptional contributions to:

- **Microbiome research** and its applications in human health
- **Innovative methodologies** for studying complex microbial communities
- **Translational research** bridging basic science and clinical applications
- **Scientific leadership** in the field of molecular ecology

## Impact and Significance

The Göran Gustafsson Prize represents:

- **Recognition of scientific excellence** at the highest level
- **Validation of our lab's research direction** and methodologies
- **Opportunity to advance** our research programs
- **Inspiration for future generations** of researchers

## Congratulations!

Congratulations, Luisa, on this well-deserved recognition! Your dedication to advancing microbiome science and improving human health through innovative research continues to inspire us all.

This award not only celebrates your individual achievements but also highlights the importance of microbiome research in modern medical science.
